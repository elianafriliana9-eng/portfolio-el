## Launch Objective

Deep Space Courier carries life-support reserves, robotics spares, and scientific cargo for AEON's long-duration crewed outposts and transit platforms.

## Readiness Notes

The cargo manifest has cleared final mass review. Thermal packaging for biological samples and cryogenic instrument shielding remain the last gated subsystems before rollout.

## Why It Matters

Reliable resupply keeps AEON's remote stations operational and reduces the risk profile for ambitious crewed missions that depend on distributed logistics rather than single-launch overprovisioning.
