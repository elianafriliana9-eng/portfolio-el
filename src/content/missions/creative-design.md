## Key Responsibilities and Achievements

- Conceptualize and execute creative visual assets for digital marketing campaigns and social media platforms to enhance brand engagement and awareness.
- Utilize professional design software (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, Canva) to develop high-quality, visually compelling marketing collateral and promotional materials.
- Maintain and evolve visual branding guidelines across all digital and print channels to ensure brand consistency and professional presentation.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align design output with broader marketing strategies and business objectives.
- Apply design thinking principles to solve communication challenges and effectively translate marketing requirements into creative solutions.
