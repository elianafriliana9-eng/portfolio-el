## Summary

AEON cryosphere analysts combined plume simulations, archival radiation exposure models, and drone deployment assumptions from Europa Recon to estimate the most probable salinity bands within sampled ejecta.

## Instrument Notes

These values are model-derived rather than directly sampled. The report exists to tune onboard software before arrival and to narrow the set of autonomous chemistry routines used during first-contact plume passes.

## Agency Implication

If confirmed in-flight, the salinity bands would support the case for active material exchange between Europa's ocean and its fractured outer shell, improving the odds that future sampling missions reach chemically informative targets.
