## Mission Objective

Europa Recon is designed to characterize the ice shell thickness, magnetic environment, and plume chemistry surrounding Jupiter's ocean moon before cryobot deployment missions are approved.

## Current Operations

The craft is midway through its outer-planet transfer profile. AEON flight dynamics teams are using cruise data to refine autonomous plume interception routines.

## Why It Matters

Europa remains one of the strongest candidates for extraterrestrial habitability in the solar system. Reconnaissance data will determine where and how AEON attempts the first controlled sub-surface access mission.
