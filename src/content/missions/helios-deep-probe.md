## Mission Objective

Helios Deep Probe studies the structure of coronal mass ejections at extreme proximity to the Sun, gathering data impossible to capture from Earth orbit.

## Current Operations

A thermal-control fault inside the forward instrument bay forced the probe into a constrained observation mode. AEON solar teams are prioritizing magnetic field telemetry and shedding nonessential payload load.

## Why It Matters

Understanding solar storm formation is essential for protecting future interplanetary crews, lunar infrastructure, and the communications mesh that ties the agency together.
